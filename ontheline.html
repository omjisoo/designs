The very first sketch involved personifying each fear and transcribing the inner conversation onto paper.

Storyboards save lives.

Character sketches:
Eye Contact, Conversation, Body Weight, Awkward Silence

In the beginning of the book, I look visibly tense because I don't know what to expect from my fears. Once I get to know them, I become more comfortable. The bare toes also signify being open and vulnerable to the inner conversations.

ON(THE)LINE

Web, Experience, 2020

ON(THE)LINE is the online capstone exhibition of VCUarts Graphic Design (GDES) Class of 2020.

Due to COVID-19, exhibition spaces and galleries are closed but it doesn't mean the show is cancelled.

The first edition was produced in limited quantities by hand with coptic stitch binding and letterpressed hardcovers. Currently I'm looking for a way to publish a second edition that will be available for more widespread distribution.
