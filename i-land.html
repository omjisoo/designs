I-land: An Atlas into the Mind

Print, Illustration, Web, 2020

How do we visualize and better understand our intangible mind?

I-land is a visual and natural representation of our mind under constant change. In the colorful guidebook, we examine the internal world and its interconnected natural system in the form of a surreal island. The book explores the relationship between each feature, as well as the natural phenomena that unfold in real time as we process the external world.

I-land: An Atlas into the Mind is now available on Blurb.

The digital edition of the original guidebook is also exhibited at ON(THE)LINE, the online capstone exhibition of VCUarts Graphic Design Class of 2020.

Visit the interactive I-land at: iland.cargo.site
