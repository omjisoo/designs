I-land: An Atlas into the Mind

Print, Illustration, Web, 2020

I-land is a visual and natural representation of our mind under constant change.

I-land: An Atlas of the Mind is a colorful guidebook that walks you through the internal world and its interconnected natural system. It examines the relationship between each feature of the I-land, as well as the natural phenomena that unfold in real time as we process the external world.


The book will be available on Blurb in May.

The digital edition of the original guidebook will also be exhibited at ON(THE)LINE, the online capstone exhibition of VCUarts Graphic Design Class of 2020.
