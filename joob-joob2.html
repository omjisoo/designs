Joob Joob

⮑ Project ⮑ Process

Joob Joob is a mobile application that helps English speakers learn the Korean language and culture. As a Joober, the user completes location-based missions in the virtual city to engage with locals and pick up practical words and expressions in the process. The user also gains interesting facts and insider tips by collecting Kollectibles after each mission.
