Joob Joob

Experience, Video, 2018

Joob Joob is a mobile app to pick up the Korean language and culture in a fun and interactive way.

The name of the app comes from the Korean slang word joobjoob (줍줍), which is an onomatopoeia for picking up items in games or memes online. As a Joober, the user completes location-based missions in the virtual city to engage with locals and pick up practical words and expressions on the way. The user also gains interesting facts and insider tips by collecting Kollectibles after each mission.

The visual elements were inspired from various traditional Korean motifs and my personal knowledge of Korea. The color scheme is based on obangsaek (오방색), the five symbolic colors, as well as my favorite shade of green from Buddhist temples.
