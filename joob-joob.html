JIS

Joob Joob is a mobile application for English speakers to learn the Korean language and culture. The name comes from the Korean internet slang 줍줍, which is a sort of onomatopoeia for picking up items in games or memes online. As a Joober, the user completes location-based missions in the virtual city to engage with locals and pick up practical words and expressions. After each mission, they unlock a Kollectible which informs interesting facts and tips about Korea.
