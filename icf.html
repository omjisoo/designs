JIS

Intercultural Festival (ICF) was an organization which coordinated cross-cultural experiences at Virginia Commonwealth University. As ICF's graphic designer, I explored a variety of styles to advertise each event with a distinct cultural theme. From 2018 to 2019, I also served as the Assistant Director and paid special attention in crafting an immersive experience for the visitors.

This proposed poster for ICF 2018 ended up not being used, but we based the final design on it.

For ICF 2019, I proposed Love, World as the theme, centering around the idea of the message traveling across the globe. As part of the campaign, four different artists (including myself) designed postcards depicting a diverse mix of terrains and culture. We distributed the postcards with questions for the visitors to contemplate on what global citizenship means to them.
