Intercultural Festival (ICF) is an annual festival celebrating cultural diversity at Virginia Commonwealth University and the Richmond community. As part of the ICF Executive Board, I also helped in coordinating multiple events to promote cross-cultural engagement. Serving as the graphic designer, I explored a variety of styles to advertise each event with a distinct cultural theme. From 2018 to 2019, I also served as the Assistant Director and paid special attention in crafting meaningful experiences for the visitors.

category

year

For the annual Intercultural Festival and smaller events throughout the years, I designed promotional materials such as posters, social media images, booklets, bus ads, t-shirts, and Snapchat filters.

Even though this proposed poster for ICF 2018 was not approved, the marketing team based the final visual identity on this concept.

For ICF 2019, I proposed Love, World as the theme, centering around the idea of messages traveling across the globe. As part of the campaign, four different artists (including myself) designed postcards depicting a diverse mix of terrains and cultures. We then distributed the postcards with questions for the visitors to contemplate on what global citizenship means to them.
