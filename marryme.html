project marry me is an interactive space for individual contemplation and community conversation on self-love. The three sections mirror three stages of the flower life cycle (seed, bud, bloom) as the metaphor of personal growth.



design

Experience, Print


year

2019


The soft carpets (to be experienced barefoot), dark green velvet curtains, and round-edged furniture create a calming and safe environment.

Upon entering the Reception Hall, you are invited to contemplate as an individual and as a community on what self-love truly means.

Once youâ€™re ready, the first step is to walk the Aisle. At first you walk in complete darkness, until the light gradually comes back on as you ascend to the next stage.

In the Ceremony Room, you renew the vows of self-love in all of your presence, with your past behind you and future in front of you.

When you exit the Ceremony Room, you step back into the Reception Hall. Here you tie a flower knot on the rope to mark this celebratory moment in your life and show your support for self-love.
