Adopt A Tote is a kiosk where grocery shoppers pick up or leave used tote bags for adoption. It encourages the shoppers to reduce plastic bag use and circulate the tote bags within the community instead of purchasing new ones. By allowing people to name each tote, the project aims to help people approach totes as a personal object and take proper care of them to use as long as possible.
