Fears Are Beings Too

⮑ Project ⮑ Process

Fears Are Beings Too is a book of conversation and reconciliation with my own fears. When I realize I have never faced my fears, I finally sit down and have a heart-to-heart talk with each of them to understand why they are still with me. In the process, I make personal discoveries.



category

Print, Illustration


year

2019

The limited edition was produced by hand with coptic stitch binding and screen printed hardcovers.

The book features eight of my fears: Eye Contact, Conversation, Body Weight, Belonging Nowhere, Asking for Help, Awkward Silence, Rejection, and Being Honest.
