Fears Are Beings Too

⮑ Project ⮑ Process

Fears Are Beings Too is a book of conversation and reconciliation with my own fears.


The book features eight fears: Eye Contact, Conversation, Body Weight, Belonging Nowhere, Asking for Help, Awkward Silence, Rejection, and Being Honest. After years of shunning the fears, I finally sit down and have a heart-to-heart talk with each of them to understand why they are still with me. In the process, I discover that they have much insight to offer.

Print, Illustration
2019

The first edition was produced in limited quantities by hand with coptic stitch binding and screen printed hardcovers. Currently I'm looking for a way to publish a second edition that will be available for more widespread distribution.
