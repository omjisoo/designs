This is the very first conversation I had with my fears, which involved personifying each fear and listening to their explanation.

In the beginning of the book, I look visibly tense because I don't know my fears well and don't know what to expect from them. As I get to know the fears through the conversations, I become more comfortable. The bare toes also signify being open and vulnerable.

Fears Are Beings Too

⮑ Project ⮑ Process

Fears Are Beings Too is a book of conversation and reconciliation with my own fears.


The book features eight fears: Eye Contact, Conversation, Body Weight, Belonging Nowhere, Asking for Help, Awkward Silence, Rejection, and Being Honest. After years of shunning the fears, I finally sit down and have a heart-to-heart talk with each of them to understand why they are still with me. In the process, I discover that they have much insight to offer.

Print, Illustration
Edition of 5
2019

The first edition was produced in limited quantities by hand with coptic stitch binding and letterpressed hardcovers. Currently I'm looking for a way to publish a second edition that will be available for more widespread distribution.
