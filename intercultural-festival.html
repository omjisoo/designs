Intercultural Festival

⮑ Project

Intercultural Festival (ICF) is an annual festival featuring various cultures from around the world.


With the United States being home to people from all over the world, the goal of ICF was to foster greater understanding of cultural diversity through family-friendly events and the annual festival. Below are a selection of graphics I created as ICF's graphic designer with each distinct culture in mind.

Print, Illustration
2017 — 2019

The theme of ICF 2018 was "One Day Around the World," based on the objective to bring different cultures together and transport festival-goers around the world for the festival day. This poster proposal was used to further develop the visual identity for the festival.

ICF also coordinated various events throughout the year that centered on a specific country or geographic region to introduce their culture to the student body at Virginia Commonwealth University. We collaborated with local restaurants, student organizations, and guest speakers to feature at the events as well.

In addition to the role of Graphic Designer, I served as the Assistant Director from 2018 to 2019 and presented the theme of ICF 2019, "Love, World," with the idea of sending the message of harmony across the globe. Four designers, including myself, came up with four postcards that unite different regions of the world together. The postcards were then handed out to visitors to write down their thoughts on global citizenship.
