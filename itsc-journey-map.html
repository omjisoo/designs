ITSC Journey Map

Print, Illustration, 2019

What does the journey map of an inexperienced IT support technician look like?

Based on my experience working at the IT Support Center (ITSC) of my university, the map visualizes the troubleshooting process as navigating through an unknown terrain. Within one call by the customer, the figurative and emotional course widely varies depending on my knowledge of the issue at hand.

The left half of the map illustrates familiar issues as a friendly neighborhood that I can lead the user through in an easy breezy stroll, while on the right side awaits an epic adventure as I attempt all sorts of troubleshooting methods.
