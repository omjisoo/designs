RBHA Meditation Labyrinths reimagine a section of the Healing Garden at the RBHA facility as a space of reflection and relaxation for their residents on recovery. Under the direction of mOb studio, an interdisciplinary design program, Gabrielle Stadulis and I developed the site plan and a 1:120 scale model of the labyrinths.
