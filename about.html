Hi, I’m Jisoo, a designer who believes in the power of stories.

I love to immerse myself in animated TV shows, feature films, and webcomics, as well as life around me. Growing up in three countries, I've also come to appreciate similarities and differences between each of our interpretations of the world. As an artist/designer, my goal is to highlight unique and heartfelt stories through vibrant colors and characters. I truly believe the more stories we know, the better we are able to understand one another.
