First Snow for Everyone

Print, Illustration, 2018

First Snow for Everyone is a wintry wrapping paper celebrating cultural diversity in America.

The moment of first snow is always magical no matter where we are from. It signals the true start of winter, of festivity and family to come together. Created in response to the theme, "The Most Wonderful Time of the Year," the entry won the Best in Show award at the 4th Annual Worth Higgins & Associates Wrapping Paper Design Contest.

The illustration depicts households that observe not only Christmas but also Hanukkah, Kwanzaa, and the Islamic tradition.

With the help of Worth Higgins & Associates, the wrapping paper came into the world in an edition of 50 rolls (6 sheets per roll). Clear varnish was applied on snowflakes and other little elements to accentuate them in the light.

Project Duration:
August 2018 - September 2018
