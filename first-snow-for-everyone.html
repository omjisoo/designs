First Snow for Everyone

⮑ Project

First Snow for Everyone is a wrapping paper with diverse ways to celebrate winter holidays.


The moment when first snow falls is always magical no matter where we are from. It signals the true start of winter, of festivity and family to come together. Created in response to the theme of "The Most Wonderful Time of the Year," the wintry wrapping paper won Best in Show at the 4th Annual Worth Higgins Associates Wrapping Paper Design Contest.

Edition of 50, 6 sheets per roll

Print, Illustration
2018

Clear varnish was applied on snowflakes and other little elements to accentuate them in the light.
