First Snow for Everyone

Print, Illustration, 2018

How can we design a more inclusive wrapping paper for winter holidays?

The moment of first snow is always magical no matter where we are from. It signals the true start of winter, of festivity and family to come together. This wintry wrapping paper includes households that observe not only Christmas but also Hanukkah, Kwanzaa, and the Islamic tradition.

With the help of Worth Higgins & Associates, the wrapping paper came into the world in an edition of 50 rolls (6 sheets per roll). Clear varnish was applied on snowflakes and other little elements to accentuate them in the light.

Project Duration:
1 month

Award:
Best in Show, 4th Annual Worth Higgins & Associates Wrapping Paper Design Contest
