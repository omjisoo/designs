My name is Jisoo, a designer and illustrator born in South Korea, raised in India, and trained in the United States. I graduated from the VCUarts Graphic Design program in May 2020 and I'm currently working as a design intern at Phenomenon.

In my spare time, I love to draw personable characters and objects that hold a story in their little quirks. I am continuously inspired by animated TV shows, films, webcomics, K-pop music videos, and artists like René Magritte.
