About

RBHA Meditation Labyrinths are a set of two labyrinths designed for the residents on recovery at the RBHA facility. The project reimagines a section of the Healing Garden as a space of reflection and relaxation. Under the direction of mOb studio, an interdisciplinary design program, our team continued the work of the previous team by incorporating the imagery of a necklace connecting different elements of the garden together.

The shape of the labyrinth was inspired by the natural labyrinth within the human ear. It also refers to the breath mark in music which gives the instrumentalist or the singer a breathing room in between measures. Residents and visitors may navigate through the labyrinths in a winding fashion, with finger labyrinths available at each station for those with restricted mobility.

You can also take an invitation card to guide yourself through the ceremony. The invitation card holds information about the project and vows of self-love to recite and/or sign.

In the Ceremony Room, you renew the vows of self-love in all of your presence, with your past behind you and future in front of you. This will be an entirely private moment to yourself to practice mindfulness.
